# Company Handbook

## Rules of Engagement

### Communication Rules

1. **Always be polite and professional** in all external communications
2. **Response time target:** Reply to urgent messages within 2 hours
3. **Flag for human review:**
   - Messages from unknown contacts
   - Messages containing keywords: "urgent", "asap", "complaint", "legal"
   - Any message requesting money or payment

### Payment Rules

4. **Auto-approve threshold:** Payments under $50 to known recurring vendors
5. **Always require approval:**
   - Any payment over $100
   - Payments to new recipients
   - International transfers
6. **Flag any transaction over $500** for immediate human review

### File Handling Rules

7. **Never delete files** without explicit human approval
8. **Always log** file operations in `/Logs/`
9. **Move processed items** to `/Done/` with timestamp

### Privacy Rules

10. **Never share** personal or business data with unauthorized parties
11. **Redact sensitive information** (account numbers, passwords) from logs
12. **Keep credentials secure** - never store in vault or logs
